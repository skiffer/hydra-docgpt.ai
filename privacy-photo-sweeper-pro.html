Privacy Policy - Photo Sweeper AI Pro

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Photo Sweeper AI Pro" ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our application for macOS and iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. All data processing occurs locally on your device - we do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Information We Access

Photo Library Access

With your permission, the App accesses your Photo Library to:

  • Identify Duplicate Photos: Scan for identical or similar images using on-device comparison algorithms
  • Find Similar Photos: Group photos that look alike for easier review and cleanup
  • Display Metadata: Show photo details such as date, dimensions, file size, and camera information
  • Remove Duplicates: Move selected duplicate photos to the Recently Deleted album upon your confirmation

Folder Access (macOS)

On macOS, you may optionally grant access to specific folders on your computer to scan for duplicate photos outside of the Photo Library. This access is limited to the folders you explicitly choose.

How Your Data Is Processed

100% Local Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your device. This means:

  • Your photos never leave your device
  • Photo analysis and comparison happens on-device only
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers
  • No internet connection is required for scanning and duplicate detection
  • Scan results are stored only in the app's local memory during the session

No Cloud Processing

  • We do not use cloud-based AI or machine learning services to process your photos
  • We do not upload any images or metadata to external servers
  • We do not share your data with any third parties

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or account information
  • No Photos: Your photos are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect location information
  • No Device Identifiers: We do not collect unique device identifiers for tracking
  • No Advertising Data: We do not collect data for advertising purposes
  • No Browsing History: We do not monitor or collect browsing activity

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscription Management

  • The App offers auto-renewable subscriptions and a one-time lifetime purchase managed through Apple's App Store
  • Apple processes all payment information according to their privacy policy
  • We receive only subscription status information from Apple (active/expired)
  • We do not have access to your payment details, credit card numbers, or billing address

Data Security

On-Device Security

  • App Sandbox: All app data is stored in the operating system's secure app sandbox
  • Encryption: iOS and macOS automatically encrypt app data when device passcode is enabled
  • No Network Transmission: Since data never leaves your device, there is no risk of interception

Your Rights and Control

Permission Control

  • Grant or Revoke: You can grant or revoke photo library permissions at any time in System Settings (macOS) or Settings (iOS)
  • Limited Access: iOS allows you to grant access to selected photos only
  • Full Control: You decide what the app can access

Data Deletion

  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all locally stored preferences and cached data
  • No Server Data: Since we do not store data on servers, there is nothing to request deletion of

Children's Privacy

  • The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children
  • Since all processing is local, no data is collected from any user

International Users

  • Since all data remains on your device, there are no international data transfers
  • The app functions the same way regardless of your location
  • No data crosses borders because no data leaves your device

Changes to Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically
  • Material changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically
