Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose Slides AI by DocGPT.AI over Beautiful.AI?

The main advantage is seamless integration - you work within Google Slides, the tool you already know, with no new platform to learn. Plus, you get unlimited presentations on the Pro plan, full animation support (which Beautiful.AI lacks), and better team pricing. If you're already using Google Workspace, DocGPT.AI is the natural choice.

Does Slides AI by DocGPT.AI support animations?

Yes! Since Slides AI by DocGPT.AI works natively within Google Slides, you have access to all of Google Slides' animation and transition features. This is a major advantage over Beautiful.AI, which doesn't support advanced animations.

Do I need to learn a new tool?

No! That's one of our biggest advantages. Beautiful.AI requires you to learn and use a completely separate platform. With Slides AI by DocGPT.AI, you simply install an add-on and continue working in Google Slides - the tool you already know. Zero learning curve.

Which is better for teams?

Slides AI by DocGPT.AI offers significantly better value for teams. Our Organization plan costs $499/month for your entire domain (unlimited users), while Beautiful.AI charges $40 per user per month. For a team of 13 or more, you'll save over 70% with DocGPT.AI.

Can I export my presentations?

Yes! Since Slides AI by DocGPT.AI creates native Google Slides presentations, you can export them to PDF, PPTX, or any format that Google Slides supports. Your presentations are fully portable.

Is there a lifetime plan?

Yes! Slides AI by DocGPT.AI offers a lifetime plan for $249 (one-time payment) with unlimited presentations and all features included forever. Beautiful.AI doesn't offer a lifetime option - only recurring subscriptions.

What about collaboration?

Since Slides AI by DocGPT.AI creates native Google Slides presentations, you get all of Google Workspace's powerful collaboration features automatically - real-time editing, commenting, sharing with Drive permissions, version history, and more. Beautiful.AI has its own collaboration system, but it's separate from your Google Workspace workflow.

Can I try before buying?

Beautiful.AI offers a 14-day free trial, while we don't currently offer a trial. However, our Starter plan at $14.99/month (150 slides) is an affordable way to test the service. Plus, with our native Google Slides integration, there's essentially no learning curve - you'll be productive immediately.

Which tool is right for me?

Choose Slides AI by DocGPT.AI if: You use Google Workspace, want unlimited presentations, prefer working in familiar tools, need animation support, want better team pricing, or prefer a lifetime payment option.

Choose Beautiful.AI if: You want a standalone platform independent of Google, need a free trial, prefer a lower monthly cost for individual use, or want their specific brand management features.
