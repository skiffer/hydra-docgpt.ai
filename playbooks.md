Playbooks are complete workflows for [GPT for Sheets](/gpt-for-sheets/) — not just a formula, but the column layout, prompt design, QA step and export. Every time figure is arithmetic from the product's rate limit (up to 10,000 results per hour), not a claimed customer result.

- **[10,000 Product Descriptions](/playbooks/10000-product-descriptions/)** — take a full catalog from bare attributes to finished copy: prompt design, sample QA, and the ~1 hour vs ~667 hours math.
- **[Lead List → Personalized Outreach](/playbooks/lead-list-to-outreach/)** — CSV in, campaign out: enrich with `=PERPLEXITY()`, score with `=GPT_CLASSIFY()`, write openers with `=GPT()`, send via mail merge.
- **[Localize a Store into 5 Languages](/playbooks/localize-store-5-languages/)** — a `=GPT_TRANSLATE()` matrix with glossary handling for brand terms and a back-translation QA trick.

Want smaller, single-formula recipes instead? Browse the [templates gallery](/templates/). New to the add-on? Start at [GPT for Sheets](/gpt-for-sheets/) — free tier, no API keys.
