Privacy Policy - Debloat Your Face with AI

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Debloat Your Face with AI" ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and other details you provide when creating an account
  • Contact Information: Email address for customer support and communication
  • Payment Information: Billing information processed through Apple's App Store for subscription management

Face Data and Biometric Information

  • Facial Images: Photos and videos you upload for AI analysis and processing
  • Food Images: Photos of food you upload for dietary analysis and debloating recommendations
  • Facial Analysis Data: Processed facial feature measurements and analysis results
  • Processing Results: Generated debloating recommendations and visual modifications

Usage Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Service Delivery: Process facial and food images to provide AI-powered debloating analysis and recommendations
  • Dietary Analysis: Analyze food images to identify potential bloating triggers and provide dietary suggestions
  • App Functionality: Enable core features, save your preferences, and maintain app performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance

Secondary Purposes

  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and user experience
  • Communication: Send important updates, security notifications, and service announcements
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights and users' safety

Face Data Processing and Storage

Important: Face data is considered sensitive biometric information. This section provides complete details on how we collect, use, store, share, and retain facial data.

What Face Data We Collect

  • Facial Photographs: Selfie images captured by you through the device camera
  • Facial Features: Measurements of facial geometry, landmarks, and bloating indicators (puffiness around eyes, cheeks, jawline)
  • Progress Images: Before/after facial images for tracking debloating progress
  • Image Metadata: Timestamp, date, and basic technical information associated with facial images
  • User Consent: All face data is collected only with your explicit consent when you actively capture a selfie in the app

How We Use Face Data

  • Bloating Analysis: Analyze facial features to identify signs of bloating, puffiness, and water retention
  • Progress Tracking: Compare your facial images over time to show debloating progress
  • Personalized Recommendations: Generate customized advice based on identified facial patterns
  • NOT Used For: Marketing, advertising, biometric authentication, or any purpose unrelated to debloating analysis

Local Processing

  • Primary facial analysis is performed locally on your device when possible
  • Local processing ensures your facial data remains on your device
  • Results are stored locally until you delete the app
  • User Control: You can delete any or all facial images at any time through the app

Cloud Processing

  • Advanced AI analysis may require temporary transmission to our secure cloud servers
  • Facial and food images are transmitted encrypted and processed immediately
  • No Permanent Storage: Images are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Images are deleted from servers immediately after processing (within 24 hours maximum)

Third-Party Sharing: Google Gemini AI

We share facial images with Google Gemini AI for processing only. Details:

  • Purpose: Google's Gemini AI API performs advanced facial analysis to identify bloating indicators
  • Data Transmitted: Facial images are sent to Google's secure servers via encrypted connection (TLS/SSL)
  • Processing Duration: Images are processed in real-time and deleted within minutes after analysis completes
  • No Permanent Storage: Google does not permanently store your facial images
  • Maximum Retention: Images are retained by Google for a maximum of 24 hours during processing, then permanently deleted
  • No Commercial Use: Your images are not used by Google for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose beyond providing the AI analysis service
  • Google's Policies: Google's handling of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy and API Terms of Service
  • No Other Third Parties: Face data is not shared with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers

Where Face Data Is Stored

  • Primary Storage: Your device's secure app sandbox (iOS encrypted storage)
  • Temporary Processing: Google Gemini AI servers (encrypted, deleted after processing)
  • Our Servers: We do not maintain long-term storage of facial images on our servers
  • Backups: If you use iCloud backup, facial images may be included in your device backup per Apple's iCloud terms

How Long We Retain Face Data

  • On Your Device: Facial images remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • During Processing: Images sent to Google Gemini AI are retained for processing duration only (minutes to maximum 24 hours), then permanently deleted
  • After App Deletion: All locally stored facial images are permanently removed when you delete the app
  • Analysis Results: Text-based analysis results (without images) may be retained for service improvement, but contain no identifiable facial data

  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

Security Limitations

  • You use the app at your own risk regarding data transmission
