Privacy Policy - Debloat Your Face with AI

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Debloat Your Face with AI" ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and other details you provide when creating an account
  • Contact Information: Email address for customer support and communication
  • Payment Information: Billing information processed through Apple's App Store for subscription management

Face Data and Biometric Information

  • Facial Images: Photos and videos you upload for AI analysis and processing
  • Food Images: Photos of food you upload for dietary analysis and debloating recommendations
  • Facial Analysis Data: Processed facial feature measurements and analysis results
  • Processing Results: Generated debloating recommendations and visual modifications

Usage Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Service Delivery: Process facial and food images to provide AI-powered debloating analysis and recommendations
  • Dietary Analysis: Analyze food images to identify potential bloating triggers and provide dietary suggestions
  • App Functionality: Enable core features, save your preferences, and maintain app performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance

Secondary Purposes

  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and user experience
  • Communication: Send important updates, security notifications, and service announcements
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights and users' safety

Face Data Processing and Storage

Local Processing

  • Primary facial analysis is performed locally on your device when possible
  • Local processing ensures your facial data remains on your device
  • Results are stored locally until you delete the app

Cloud Processing

  • Advanced AI analysis may require temporary transmission to our secure cloud servers
  • Facial and food images are transmitted encrypted and processed immediately
  • No Permanent Storage: Images are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Images are deleted from servers immediately after processing (within 24 hours maximum)

Third-Party AI Services

  • Google Gemini AI: We use Google's Gemini AI API for advanced facial and food image analysis
  • Data Processing: Images are transmitted to Google's secure servers for AI processing
  • Temporary Processing: Image data transmitted to Gemini AI is processed temporarily and not stored permanently
  • Google's Privacy Policy: Google's handling of your data is governed by their privacy policy and terms of service
  • No Commercial Use: Your images are not used by Google for commercial purposes beyond providing the AI analysis service
  • Data Deletion: Images are deleted from Google's systems after processing according to their data retention policies

Apple App Store Integration

Subscription Management

  • Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store
  • Apple processes all payment information according to their privacy policy
  • We receive limited subscription status information from Apple

Data Sharing with Apple

  • We may share consumption and purchase data with Apple as required for refund processing
  • This sharing is limited to transaction-related information only
  • Your consent to this sharing is required for App Store compliance

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

Security Limitations

  • No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security but use industry-standard practices
  • You use the app at your own risk regarding data transmission

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access and Control

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Opt-Out Options

  • Marketing Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Data Collection: Limit certain data collection through app settings
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion
  • Images (Facial and Food): Not permanently stored; deleted after processing
  • Gemini AI Processing: Images sent to Google Gemini AI are processed and deleted according to Google's data retention policies
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for service improvement purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Children's Privacy

  • The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us

International Data Transfers

  • Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your residence
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers
  • Data protection standards equivalent to your home country are maintained

Changes to Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically
  • Material changes will be notified through the app or via email
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Previous versions are available upon request

  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
