Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Rectangle Pro & Pin Window

This Privacy Policy describes how Rectangle Pro & Pin Window ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Rectangle Pro & Pin Window is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Data Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored only on your device:

  • Your keyboard shortcut preferences
  • Window snapping settings and configurations
  • Snap area customizations
  • Launch at login preference
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Window contents or screenshots
  • Application usage data from other apps
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any form of behavioral data

1.4 Purchase Information

If you purchase Rectangle Pro & Pin Window through the Mac App Store:

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID
  • Purchase receipts are validated locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework

2. System Permissions

Rectangle Pro & Pin Window requires certain system permissions to function. Here's what each permission is used for and how your privacy is protected:

2.1 Accessibility Permission

What it's used for:

  • Moving and resizing windows from any application
  • Detecting the currently focused window
  • Applying window snapping actions (halves, quarters, etc.)

Privacy protection: The Accessibility API is used solely to read window positions and sizes, and to move/resize windows. We do not read window contents, text, or any other information from your applications.

2.2 Screen Recording Permission

What it's used for:

  • The "Pin Window" feature captures a visual preview of the selected window
  • Creating an always-on-top floating preview that stays visible while you work

Privacy protection:

  • Window captures are processed entirely in memory on your device
  • Captured images are never saved to disk, transmitted, or stored permanently
  • When you unpin a window, the captured image is immediately discarded
  • We have no ability to access or view any captured content

3. How the App Works

Rectangle Pro & Pin Window functions entirely offline and locally:

  • Window Management: Uses macOS Accessibility API to move and resize windows
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Global hotkeys are registered locally with the system
  • Snap Areas: Drag-to-snap zones are processed locally using mouse position
  • Pin Window: Window previews are captured and displayed using local graphics APIs
  • Settings: All preferences are stored in local UserDefaults on your Mac

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected by Apple's built-in security measures:

  • All app data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • Data is protected by your Mac's encryption (FileVault if enabled)
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your Mac

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses only the following Apple services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple StoreKit: For purchase validation

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Rectangle Pro & Pin Window is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or external links beyond this privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems
  • All your preferences and data are stored locally on your Mac
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • You can reset app settings within the app at any time
  • You can revoke Accessibility or Screen Recording permissions at any time in System Settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Since Rectangle Pro & Pin Window does not collect any personal information, these rights are automatically fulfilled. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area: Rectangle Pro & Pin Window does not collect or process personal data. All app functionality operates locally on your device without any data transmission to external servers, fully complying with GDPR requirements.

The Accessibility and Screen Recording permissions are used solely to provide window management features - we never access, store, or transmit window contents or any other data from your applications.
