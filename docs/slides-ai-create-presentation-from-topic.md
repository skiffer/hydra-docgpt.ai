# Create a presentation from a topic

The core feature of <a href="/docs/slides-ai">Slides AI by DocGPT</a> is generating a complete Google Slides deck from a single topic. Open your presentation, go to **Extensions → AI GPT for Slides → Start**, type a topic, set the options, and generate.

## Writing a good topic

The topic field steers everything the AI writes, so be specific:

- **Name the subject and the angle.** "Remote onboarding checklist for engineering managers" produces a more focused deck than "Onboarding".
- **Include the audience when it matters.** "Explaining GDPR basics to a marketing team" gets simpler wording than the topic alone.
- **State the goal if there is one.** "Pitch: why we should switch to quarterly planning" tells the AI to argue a position, not just describe one.
- **Keep it to one subject.** If you need two subjects, generate two decks or add detail through the additional content field — see <a href="/docs/slides-ai/create-slides-from-text">Create slides from text</a>.

## Choosing the number of slides

You set the slide count before generating. As a rule of thumb:

- **5–7 slides** — a short update, a single idea, a lightning talk.
- **8–12 slides** — a standard meeting presentation with room for context and next steps.
- **13+ slides** — training material or a detailed walkthrough; consider splitting very broad subjects into separate decks instead.

Fewer slides give the AI more room per slide; more slides spread the topic thinner, so broad topics fit longer decks better than narrow ones.

## What the generator produces

Every generated deck is built from three layout types:

1. **Title slide** — a title and subtitle for the deck.
2. **Bullet-point content slides** — the body of the presentation.
3. **Image + text slides** — mixed layouts that pair a visual with supporting text.

All of it consists of native Google Slides objects: you can reword text, move elements, change the theme, and export to PPTX or PDF via the normal **File → Download** menu.

## Example topics with recommended settings

| Topic | Slides | Tone | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| "Q3 marketing results and Q4 priorities for the leadership team" | 8 | Professional | Add your actual numbers via the additional content field |
| "Introduction to composting for a community workshop" | 10 | Friendly | Broad how-to subject, benefits from more slides |
| "Pitch: subscription pricing for our mobile app" | 6 | Persuasive | Short and focused works best for pitches |
| "New employee IT security training" | 12 | Formal | Training content; longer deck, one rule per slide |
| "Nuestro plan de lanzamiento para el mercado español" | 8 | Professional | Set the output language to Spanish to generate the deck natively |

Tone names vary by add-on version — pick the closest option in the sidebar. For tone guidance and the full language workflow, see <a href="/docs/slides-ai/writing-tones-and-languages">Writing tones and languages</a>.
