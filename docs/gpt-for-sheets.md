## GPT Functions

The add-on provides a variety of GPT functions tailored for different use cases. You can explore them by typing `=GPT_` in a cell.

Particularly useful GPT functions for Google Sheets include:

- GPT
- GPT_LIST
- GPT_SPLIT
- GPT_FILL
- GPT_TABLE
- GPT_FORMAT
- GPT_EDIT
- GPT_TAG
- GPT_CLASSIFY
- GPT_EXTRACT
- GPT_SUMMARIZE
- GPT_TRANSLATE
