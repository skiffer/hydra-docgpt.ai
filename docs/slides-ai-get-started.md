# Get started with Slides AI by DocGPT

This guide takes you from installation to your first AI-generated deck. The add-on is listed in the Google Workspace Marketplace as **AI GPT for Slides™** (marketed as <a href="/google-slide-ai/">Slides by DocGPT.ai</a>).

## Install from the Marketplace

1. Open the <a href="https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/create_presentation_with_ai_chatgpt_slid/517982554715">AI GPT for Slides listing</a> in the Google Workspace Marketplace.
2. Click **Install** and pick the Google account you use with Google Slides.
3. Review and approve the requested permissions. The add-on needs access to your presentations so it can insert the slides it generates.

No credit card is required — the free plan includes 10 AI presentations per month.

## Find the menu

After installation, open any Google Slides presentation and go to:

**Extensions → AI GPT for Slides → Start**

This opens the add-on sidebar. If the menu item is missing, refresh the browser tab and confirm you are signed in with the account that installed the add-on — see <a href="/docs/slides-ai/credits-and-troubleshooting">Credits and troubleshooting</a> for details.

## Generate your first deck in 3 steps

1. **Enter a topic.** Type what the presentation should be about, for example "Onboarding plan for new sales hires".
2. **Set the options.** Choose the number of slides, the writing tone, and the output language. Optionally paste your own text or outline into the additional content field.
3. **Generate.** The add-on builds the deck: a title slide, bullet-point content slides, and image + text slides. All slides are native Google Slides objects, so you can edit them immediately or export to PPTX/PDF via **File → Download**.

You can also use the **Ask AI** feature to send a freeform prompt and insert the answer into the deck.

## Free plan limits

The free plan includes 10 AI presentations per month. When you reach the limit, generation pauses until the next month or until you upgrade — paid plans start at $14.99/mo, see <a href="/google-slide-ai/#pricing">pricing</a>. How credits work is covered in <a href="/docs/slides-ai/credits-and-troubleshooting">Credits and troubleshooting</a>.
