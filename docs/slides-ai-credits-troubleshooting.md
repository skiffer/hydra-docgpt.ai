# Credits and troubleshooting

This page covers how the free plan of <a href="/docs/slides-ai">Slides AI by DocGPT</a> works, what to do when you run out of credits, and fixes for the most common add-on issues.

## How free credits work

- The free plan includes **10 AI presentations per month**.
- No credit card is required — the free allowance is active as soon as you <a href="https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/create_presentation_with_ai_chatgpt_slid/517982554715">install the add-on</a>.
- Each generated presentation counts against the monthly allowance.

## When credits run out

When you use up the monthly allowance, generation pauses until the allowance renews the next month. To keep generating without waiting, upgrade to a paid plan — plans start at $14.99/mo. Current tiers and what they include are listed on the <a href="/google-slide-ai/#pricing">pricing section of the Slides AI page</a>.

## Common issues

### The add-on is not visible in the Extensions menu

1. Refresh the browser tab with your presentation — the Extensions menu is only rebuilt on page load.
2. Confirm you are signed in with the Google account that installed the add-on. Check the account avatar in the top-right corner of Google Slides.
3. If it is still missing, reinstall from the <a href="https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/create_presentation_with_ai_chatgpt_slid/517982554715">Marketplace listing</a> while signed in with the correct account.

### Generation fails or never finishes

1. Retry the generation — transient failures usually succeed on a second attempt.
2. Shorten the topic. Very long topics (or very large pasted text in the additional content field) are the most common cause of failed runs; trim the input and try again. See <a href="/docs/slides-ai/create-slides-from-text">Create slides from text</a> for tips on preparing source text.
3. Reduce the slide count and generate again.

### Signed in to multiple Google accounts

Google Slides add-ons can misbehave when several Google accounts are signed in to the same browser session: the add-on may run under a different account than the presentation. Workarounds:

- Open the presentation from your **default account** — the one at `https://docs.google.com/presentation/u/0/`.
- Or sign out of the extra accounts, or use a separate browser profile (or an incognito window) with only the account that installed the add-on.

## Where to get support

If none of the above helps, see the general <a href="/docs/support-gpt-sheets-docs-slides-forms-gmail/">Troubleshooting GPT for Google Sheets, Docs, Slides, Forms, Gmail</a> section, which includes contact options for the DocGPT.ai support team.
