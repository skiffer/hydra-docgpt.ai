# Slides AI by DocGPT

### Links:
- <a href="https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/create_presentation_with_ai_chatgpt_slid/517982554715">Install Slides by DocGPT.ai</a> from the Google Workspace Marketplace
- <a href="/google-slide-ai/">Slides AI landing page</a> — features and pricing

<a href="/google-slide-ai/">Slides AI by DocGPT</a> (add-on name: AI GPT for Slides™) generates a complete Google Slides presentation from a topic you type. You choose the number of slides, the writing tone, and the output language (40+ languages), and you can paste your own text, outline, or notes for the AI to use as source material.

Generated decks include a title slide with a title and subtitle, bullet-point content slides, and image + text layout slides. Every slide is a native Google Slides object, so you can edit the result freely and export it to PPTX or PDF through the normal Google Slides File menu. An additional **Ask AI** feature lets you send a freeform prompt and insert the answer into the deck.

The add-on is part of the DocGPT.ai add-on suite used by 500,000+ users. The free plan includes 10 AI presentations per month with no credit card required; paid plans start at $14.99/mo — see <a href="/google-slide-ai/#pricing">pricing</a>.

## How to install

1. Open the <a href="https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/create_presentation_with_ai_chatgpt_slid/517982554715">Google Workspace Marketplace listing</a> and click **Install**.
2. Approve the requested permissions for the Google account you use with Google Slides.
3. Open any Google Slides presentation and go to **Extensions → AI GPT for Slides → Start**.

For a step-by-step walkthrough, see <a href="/docs/slides-ai/get-started">Get started</a>.
