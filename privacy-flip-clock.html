Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2025

Flip Clock & Digital Widgets

This Privacy Policy describes how Flip Clock & Digital Widgets ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our iOS and macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Flip Clock & Digital Widgets is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Data Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored only on your device:

  • Your theme preferences and customizations
  • Timer and countdown settings
  • Pomodoro session configurations
  • Widget display preferences
  • App settings (12/24 hour format, date display options)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing or search history
  • Any form of behavioral data

1.4 Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases (such as premium themes):

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID
  • Purchase receipts are validated locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework

2. How the App Works

Flip Clock & Digital Widgets functions entirely offline and locally:

  • Clock Display: Shows current time using your device's system clock
  • Timers & Countdowns: All timing functions run locally on your device
  • Pomodoro Timer: Focus sessions are tracked only on your device
  • Widgets: Home screen widgets display time using iOS/macOS widget extensions
  • Themes: All visual customizations are stored in local app storage
  • Notifications: Timer notifications are handled by the local system notification service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected by Apple's built-in security measures:

  • All app data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • Data is protected by your device's encryption
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses only the following Apple services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases
  • Apple WidgetKit: For home screen widget functionality
  • Apple Notifications: For timer alerts (with your permission)

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

5. Permissions

Flip Clock & Digital Widgets may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when timers complete (optional)

You can manage these permissions at any time in your device's Settings app.

6. Children's Privacy

Flip Clock & Digital Widgets is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or external links beyond this privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems
  • All your preferences and data are stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • You can reset app settings within the app at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@docgpt.ai
  • Website: https://docgpt.ai

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Since Flip Clock & Digital Widgets does not collect any personal information, these rights are automatically fulfilled. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area: Flip Clock & Digital Widgets does not collect or process personal data. All app functionality operates locally on your device without any data transmission to external servers, fully complying with GDPR requirements.

Summary: Flip Clock & Digital Widgets respects your privacy. The app works entirely offline on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your settings and preferences stay on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.
