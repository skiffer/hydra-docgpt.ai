## How to move an outreach list from a CRM or CSV into Google Sheets

If your contacts live in a CRM or a CSV export today:

1. Export your contacts to CSV from your CRM.
2. In Google Sheets, choose **File → Import** and upload the CSV.
3. Clean the columns: keep `email`, `first_name`, `company`, and any personalization fields you need.
4. Add a `status` column to track draft, tested, sent, replied, and bounced rows.
5. Remove duplicates and obviously invalid addresses before sending.

## Step-by-step: your first personalized campaign

1. Open the Google Sheet that holds your list and merge fields.
2. Install and open **Mail Merge for Gmail and Google Sheets**.
3. Map your columns to merge tags such as `{{first_name}}` and `{{company}}`.
4. Write a template in Gmail that reads naturally when personalized.
5. Send a **test message** to yourself and a teammate and review every merge field.
6. Send in small batches first, then scale while monitoring replies and bounces.

## Sending-quality checklist

- Email only consented or clearly relevant recipients.
- Run a test send and verify each merge tag before scaling.
- Respect **Gmail and Google Workspace sending limits** and pace larger sends.
- Include an opt-out or unsubscribe option where applicable.
- Monitor bounces and replies, and clean your list as you go.
- Keep copy genuine and specific rather than generic or spammy.
