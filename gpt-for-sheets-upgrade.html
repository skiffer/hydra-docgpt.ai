Quick answers

Which plan should I choose if I clicked Unlock Chat?

Choose Pro User or Business. AI Chat is available on Pro User and Business plans.

Do I need an API key?

Starter does not require an API key. Pro User and Business unlock advanced models and custom model control, so you should configure your provider API keys after purchase.

Do I need to install the add-on again?

No. After purchase, return to Google Sheets and click Extensions > GPT for Sheets > Refresh Account.

What if I use the wrong email?

Use the same Google email you use with the add-on. If you purchased with the wrong email, contact support so the subscription can be matched or transferred.

What is the fastest way to activate after purchase?

Open Google Sheets, then click Extensions > GPT for Sheets > Refresh Account. Your plan should update in the sidebar after refresh.

What does Starter include?

Starter is for core GPT spreadsheet functions with included monthly requests and no API key setup. It is best for everyday formulas, summaries, extraction, classification, and translation.

Why upgrade to Pro User?

Pro User unlocks AI Chat, advanced models, image and vision tools, time-based automation triggers, and custom API key support.

When should I choose Business?

Choose Business for unlimited or high-volume workflows, lead enrichment, email tools, indexing, SEO, YouTube, Google Maps, and priority support.

Can I keep using existing formulas?

Yes. Your existing sheet formulas keep working. Upgrading removes limits and unlocks more tools without changing your spreadsheet setup.

Does upgrading give me more models?

Yes. Pro User and Business support advanced model selection with your own API keys, including OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Mistral, Perplexity, and other supported providers.

Are image generation tools included?

Image generation and vision tools are available on Pro User and Business. Some image models may require a paid provider account or organization verification from the model provider.

Will it work for bulk rows?

Yes. Paid plans are designed for spreadsheet workflows across many rows. Business is the best fit when you process large batches regularly.

Can I cancel?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription from the billing portal or contact support for help.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. There is a 30-day money-back guarantee if the plan is not right for your workflow.

Will my team need separate seats?

Business is the best plan for team or business workflows. If you need multiple users, contact support so the setup matches how your team works.
