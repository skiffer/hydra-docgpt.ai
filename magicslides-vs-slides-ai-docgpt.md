## Short answer {#short-answer}

**MagicSlides and Slides by DocGPT.ai are both Google Slides add-ons**, so either way your deck is generated as a native, editable Google Slides file — no separate platform, and normal Drive sharing and version history apply.

The core trade-off: **MagicSlides is built around breadth of inputs** — its site advertises converting text, topics, YouTube videos, PDFs, URLs, images, and even audio into slides — with paid plans starting at **$29/mo**. **Slides by DocGPT.ai is built around a simple topic-to-deck workflow** — topic or pasted text in, complete deck out, with slide count, writing tone, and 40+ languages — with a **free tier of 10 presentations per month** and paid plans from **$14.99/mo**.

## When to choose MagicSlides {#when-magicslides}

MagicSlides is a strong pick if:

- **You convert existing content into slides.** Its converters for YouTube videos, PDFs, URLs, images, video, and audio are the product's signature feature — useful if your decks usually start from source material rather than a blank topic.
- **You generate at high volume.** Premium Plus ($34/mo) offers unlimited presentations, and Premium allows up to 50 slides per deck.
- **You want done-for-you decks.** A "Done By Us" service tier (listed at $200 one-time) has their team build the presentation for you.
- **Scale reassures you.** The site claims 2.3M+ users and 10M+ presentations created.

## When to choose Slides by DocGPT.ai {#when-docgpt}

Slides by DocGPT.ai is the better fit if:

- **You want a clearly defined free tier.** 10 free presentations per month, stated up front — enough for regular occasional use without a paid plan.
- **You want a lower paid entry point.** Plans start at $14.99/mo versus MagicSlides' $29/mo Premium — see [current pricing](/google-slide-ai/#pricing).
- **Your decks start from a topic or your own text.** Enter a topic or paste source text, set slide count, tone, and one of 40+ languages, and get a complete deck you edit immediately in Slides.
- **You value staying native.** The deck belongs to your Google Drive from the moment it's generated, with normal collaboration, sharing, and versioning. It is part of the DocGPT.ai suite used by 500,000+ users.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the main difference between MagicSlides and Slides by DocGPT.ai?

Both are Google Slides add-ons that generate native, editable decks. MagicSlides focuses on converting many input formats — YouTube videos, PDFs, URLs, images, audio — into slides, with paid plans from $29/mo. Slides by DocGPT.ai focuses on a direct topic-to-deck workflow with slide count, tone, and 40+ language controls, a free tier of 10 presentations per month, and paid plans from $14.99/mo.

### Which has the better free tier?

Slides by DocGPT.ai states its free tier plainly: 10 presentations per month at no cost. MagicSlides is free to start with no credit card, but its pricing page doesn't list the current free-tier limits, so check their site before relying on it.

### Which is cheaper for paid use?

Slides by DocGPT.ai's paid plans start at $14.99/mo. MagicSlides' Premium plan is $29/mo (annual billing saves 17%), and its unlimited Premium Plus tier is $34/mo. If you need MagicSlides' YouTube/PDF conversion features, that premium may be worth it; if you mostly build decks from topics or your own text, the lower entry price wins.
