# How to Promote an Ebook with Gmail Mail Merge and Google Sheets

Launching an ebook does not require a complicated marketing stack. If your readers, followers, clients, or webinar attendees are already in a spreadsheet, **Mail Merge for Gmail and Google Sheets** gives you a simple way to send personalized launch emails, reminders, PDF links or attachments, and follow-ups from Gmail.

[Start your ebook launch campaign →](/mail-merge-for-gmail-and-sheets/)

## Who this workflow is for

This is useful for:

- nutritionists promoting a meal plan or wellness guide;
- psychologists or coaches sharing worksheets or paid resources;
- creators launching a guide to an Instagram audience;
- educators selling templates or workbooks;
- consultants sending a practical PDF to leads;
- small businesses announcing a digital product to existing customers.

The workflow is simple: keep your launch list in Google Sheets, write a Gmail-style message, personalize each email, and track who needs a follow-up.

## Build your ebook launch Sheet

Create a Sheet with columns like:

| Column | Example |
|---|---|
| `email` | reader@example.com |
| `first_name` | Alex |
| `source` | Instagram, newsletter, webinar |
| `segment` | beginner, buyer, waitlist, past client |
| `interest` | meal prep, anxiety toolkit, lead generation |
| `ebook_title` | 7-Day Meal Prep Guide |
| `cta_link` | checkout or download link |
| `status` | announcement sent, clicked, bought, follow-up |
| `follow_up_note` | asked for sample chapter |

This keeps the campaign practical: every row is a person, every column can power a more relevant message.

## Three-email ebook launch sequence

### 1. Announcement email

Subject: `{{first_name}}, the {{ebook_title}} is ready`

Hi {{first_name}},

You joined my list through {{source}}, so I wanted to let you know that {{ebook_title}} is now available.

It is built for people interested in {{interest}} and includes practical steps you can use right away.

Get it here: {{cta_link}}

### 2. Value email

Subject: `One useful idea from {{ebook_title}}`

Hi {{first_name}},

One idea from the guide: {{sample_tip}}.

If that is useful, the full {{ebook_title}} includes the full checklist and examples.

Here is the link: {{cta_link}}

### 3. Reminder email

Subject: `Last reminder about {{ebook_title}}`

Hi {{first_name}},

Quick reminder that {{ebook_title}} is available here: {{cta_link}}.

If you are focused on {{interest}}, this is the fastest way to get the full resource in one place.

## Send a PDF attachment or a secure link?

For many ebook campaigns, a secure link is better than attaching a large PDF to every email. Use an attachment when the recipient expects the file and you have tested deliverability. Use a link when the file may be updated, when access should be controlled, or when the PDF is large.

Related guide: [Mail merge with attachments in Gmail and Google Sheets](/mail-merge-with-attachments-gmail-google-sheets/).

## Follow up without losing context

After the first send, use the Sheet to decide the next action:

- clicked but did not buy → send a reminder or sample chapter;
- replied with a question → send a personal response;
- bought → send onboarding or bonus content;
- no engagement → wait, segment, or skip;
- bounced/unsubscribed → remove from future sends.

[Use Mail Merge to track and follow up →](/mail-merge-for-gmail-and-sheets/)

## Sending-quality checklist

- Send to people who opted in or have a clear relationship with you.
- Use one main CTA per email.
- Make the sender identity obvious.
- Test every merge field.
- Send a small test segment first.
- Respect Gmail and Workspace limits.
- Monitor replies, bounces, and opt-outs.

## Ebook mail merge FAQ

### Can I promote an ebook using Gmail?

Yes. Gmail works well for small ebook launches when your audience is permissioned and the campaign is personalized.

### Why use Google Sheets for an ebook launch?

Sheets keeps your readers, segments, interests, launch status, and follow-up notes visible in one place. That makes it easier to personalize without a complex CRM.

### Should I attach the ebook PDF?

Sometimes, but a secure link is often safer for large or updated files. If you attach PDFs, test delivery and make sure recipients expect the file.

### What should I send after the launch email?

Send a value email, reminder, sample chapter, bonus resource, or follow-up based on clicks, replies, and buyer status.

### Can this work for Instagram audiences?

Yes. Collect interested followers into a Sheet through a form, lead magnet, webinar, or waitlist, then send a focused Gmail campaign.
