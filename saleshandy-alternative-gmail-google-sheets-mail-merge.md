# A Saleshandy Alternative for Gmail Mail Merge from Google Sheets

If your outreach list already lives in Google Sheets, a full cold-email and sequencing platform can be more than your workflow needs. **Mail Merge for Gmail and Google Sheets** lets you send personalized Gmail campaigns straight from a spreadsheet — a focused, Workspace-native option for people searching for a **Saleshandy alternative** built around mail merge.

## Why people look for a Saleshandy alternative

People typically start shopping for an alternative when:

- their contact list already lives in **Google Sheets**, and exporting to CSV each time feels like friction;
- they want personalized Gmail sending without adopting a broader sequencing suite;
- they need a simpler setup for a solo founder, a single SDR, or a small sales team;
- they are weighing cost against how many platform features they will actually use.

None of this makes Saleshandy a poor product — it just means the job some people are hiring for is "send a personalized campaign from my Sheet," not "run a full multi-account sequencing platform."

## Saleshandy in context

Saleshandy is generally positioned as a **cold-email and sales-sequencing platform**, with features oriented around multi-step sequences, email warm-up, and sender rotation across multiple mailboxes. As of 2026 its paid plans start around the mid-twenties of dollars per month, but pricing and features change over time, so confirm the current details on Saleshandy's own site before deciding.

If you need a dedicated outbound engine with sequences, warm-up, and rotation across many sending accounts, a platform like that may be the right call. If your real need is personalized Gmail sending from a spreadsheet, a mail merge tool is a closer fit.

## How the Mail Merge workflow works

1. Open the Google Sheet that holds your list and merge fields.
2. Install and open **Mail Merge for Gmail and Google Sheets**.
3. Map your columns to merge tags such as `{{first_name}}` and `{{company}}`.
4. Write a Gmail template that reads naturally when personalized.
5. Send a **test message** to yourself and a teammate and review every merge field.
6. Send in small batches first, then scale while monitoring replies and bounces.
7. Add follow-up sends for recipients who have not replied.

## What you keep, and what you trade

Moving from a sequencing suite to a Sheets-native mail merge, you keep personalization, attachments where supported, tracking where supported, follow-ups, and sending from your own mailbox. You trade away heavier automation such as fully automated multi-step sequences, built-in warm-up, and managed rotation across many accounts. For many spreadsheet-first senders that trade is worth a lighter tool and a simpler cost structure.

## Pricing and fit considerations

Keep cost comparisons grounded in what you actually send. A per-seat sequencing platform can make sense at higher outbound volume across a team; a spreadsheet-driven mail merge often fits solo senders and small teams running periodic, highly personalized campaigns. Always confirm both vendors' current pricing on their own sites, since plans change.

## Sending-quality checklist

- Email only consented or clearly relevant recipients.
- Run a test send and verify each merge tag before scaling.
- Respect **Gmail and Google Workspace sending limits** and pace larger sends.
- Include an opt-out or unsubscribe option where applicable.
- Monitor bounces and replies, and clean your list as you go.
- Keep copy genuine and specific rather than generic or spammy.

## Saleshandy alternative FAQ

### Is Mail Merge for Gmail and Sheets a Saleshandy alternative?

It can be, depending on your goal. If you mainly want personalized Gmail campaigns sent from a Google Sheet, a mail merge tool covers that job with a lighter setup. If you need a full cold-email sequencing platform with warm-up and rotation, compare dedicated suites too.

### Does Saleshandy work directly with Google Sheets?

Publicly available information indicates Saleshandy typically works from imported contacts rather than a live Google Sheet. Check Saleshandy's current documentation to confirm, since features change.

### Can I keep using my own Gmail address?

Yes. Mail Merge for Gmail and Sheets sends from your own Gmail or Google Workspace mailbox, so replies come back to you.

No tool can guarantee inbox placement. Genuine personalization, a clean list, sensible pacing, and test sends all improve your odds.
