**DocGPT.ai** is a developer of AI add-ons for Google Workspace. Our tools bring GPT, Claude, Gemini, Mistral and Perplexity models directly into Google Sheets, Gmail, Docs, Slides and Forms — with the models built in, so no API keys are required to get started. The brand name is written **DocGPT.ai**; you may also see it referred to as DocGPT or DocGPT AI. It is not affiliated with document-chat products that use similar names.

## What we build

- **[GPT for Sheets](/gpt-for-sheets/)** — the AI agent for bulk tasks in Google Sheets: content generation, translation, classification, data cleaning, web search and data extraction with formulas like `=GPT()`, `=CLAUDE()`, `=GEMINI()`, `=PERPLEXITY()`. Processes up to 10,000 results per hour.
- **[Mail Merge for Gmail](/mail-merge-for-gmail-and-sheets/)** — personalized email campaigns from Google Sheets with AI-assisted writing.
- **[AI Email Assistant](/ai-email-assistant/)** — AI-powered composition and replies inside Gmail.
- **[GPT for Docs](/google-doc-ai/)** — AI writing assistant for Google Docs.
- **[Google Slide AI](/google-slide-ai/)** — presentation generation and translation for Google Slides.
- **[Quiz Maker AI](/quiz-maker-ai/)** — automated quiz and assessment generation for Google Forms.

The add-ons are distributed through the [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/gpt_for_sheets_docs_forms_slides/466607203252), where GPT for Sheets is rated 4.8/5, and the suite has passed Google's OAuth verification and review process.

## Not to be confused with

Several unrelated products share a similar name. **DocGPT.ai** (this site) builds AI add-ons for Google Workspace. We are not affiliated with:

- **docgpt.io** — a web-based PDF and document-chat editor
- **DocGPT Health** (docgpt.health) — an AI health assistant
- **theDocGPT** (thedocgpt.com) — a PDF summarizer
- **DocGPT by Amitree/Folio** — a document tool for email inboxes
- **docGPT open-source project** on GitHub — a community Google Docs script

If you are looking for AI formulas in Google Sheets, AI presentations in Google Slides, or mail merge in Gmail — you are in the right place: that is DocGPT.ai.

## How our tools handle data

Our add-ons run inside your Google account through the official Google Workspace add-on framework and request only the OAuth scopes needed to work with the current document. Prompt content is sent to the selected AI model provider to generate results; we do not sell user data. Details are in the [privacy policy](/privacy/).
