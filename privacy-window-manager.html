Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro

This Privacy Policy describes how Pin Window: Rectangle Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our macOS application available on the Mac App Store.

Quick Summary: Pin Window: Rectangle Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works 100% offline on your Mac.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Data Collection

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any data from you. The app operates entirely on your local device with no network connectivity required.

1.2 App Privacy Label (App Store)

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, our App Privacy "Nutrition Label" accurately reflects that:

  • Data Not Collected: We do not collect any data from this app
  • Data Not Linked to You: No data is linked to your identity
  • No Tracking: We do not track you across apps or websites

1.3 Local Data Only

The following data is stored only on your device in the app's sandboxed container:

  • Pin window behavior preferences
  • Launch at login setting
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Window contents, screenshots, or screen recordings
  • Application usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, serial numbers, etc.)
  • Crash logs or diagnostics
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data
  • Any data from other applications

2. Screen Recording Permission

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro requires Screen Recording permission. This section explains exactly why and how this permission is used.

2.1 Why We Need This Permission

The Screen Recording permission is required by macOS to use Apple's ScreenCaptureKit framework. This is the only way to capture a window's visual appearance on macOS. We use this to:

  • Capture a still image of the window you choose to pin
  • Display that image as a floating always-on-top preview
  • Periodically refresh the preview to show updated content

2.2 What We Capture

  • Only the specific window you select - we do not capture your entire screen
  • Still images only - no video recording occurs
  • No audio - we never capture any audio

2.3 Privacy Protections

  • Captures exist only in temporary memory (RAM)
  • Captures are never saved to disk
  • Captures are never transmitted over the network
  • Captures are immediately discarded when you unpin a window
  • We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve any captured content
  • The app has no network capabilities - it cannot send data anywhere

3. How the App Works

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro is a fully offline utility:

  • Pin Window Feature: Uses Apple's ScreenCaptureKit API to capture window images
  • Floating Preview: Displays the captured image in an NSPanel window overlay
  • Settings Storage: Uses macOS UserDefaults (local storage only)
  • No Internet: The app makes no network connections whatsoever

4. Data Retention

  • Window captures: Retained only in memory while a window is pinned; immediately discarded when unpinned
  • Preferences: Stored locally until you uninstall the app or manually reset settings
  • No server-side data: We have no servers and retain no data about you

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone. Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share. We do not:

  • Sell data to third parties
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use any analytics services
  • Integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data

6. Third-Party Services

The app integrates only with Apple services:

  • Mac App Store: For app distribution, updates, and purchase processing

Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by macOS security features:

  • App runs in a sandboxed environment
  • Local data is protected by macOS security and FileVault encryption (if enabled)
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated local data

8. Children's Privacy
