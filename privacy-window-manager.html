Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro

This Privacy Policy describes how Pin Window: Rectangle Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Data Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored only on your device:

  • Your pin window preferences
  • Launch at login preference
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Window contents or screenshots
  • Application usage data from other apps
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any form of behavioral data

1.4 Purchase Information

If you purchase Pin Window: Rectangle Pro through the Mac App Store:

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID
  • Purchase receipts are validated locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework

2. System Permissions

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro requires the Screen Recording permission to function. Here's what this permission is used for and how your privacy is protected:

2.1 Screen Recording Permission

What it's used for:

  • Capturing a visual preview of the window you select to pin
  • Creating an always-on-top floating preview that stays visible while you work
  • Refreshing the pinned window preview to show current content

Privacy protection:

  • We only capture the specific window you choose to pin - nothing else
  • Window captures are processed entirely in memory on your device
  • Captured images are never saved to disk, transmitted, or stored permanently
  • When you unpin a window, the captured image is immediately discarded
  • We have no ability to access or view any captured content
  • No audio is captured - only still images for the visual preview

3. How the App Works

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro functions entirely offline and locally:

  • Pin Window: Uses Apple's ScreenCaptureKit to capture window previews displayed using local graphics APIs
  • Floating Preview: The pinned window preview is displayed as a local NSPanel overlay
  • Settings: All preferences are stored in local UserDefaults on your Mac
  • No Network Access: The app does not connect to the internet for any functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected by Apple's built-in security measures:

  • All app data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • Data is protected by your Mac's encryption (FileVault if enabled)
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your Mac

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses only the following Apple services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple StoreKit: For purchase validation

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Pin Window: Rectangle Pro is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or external links beyond this privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems
  • All your preferences and data are stored locally on your Mac
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • You can reset app settings within the app at any time
  • You can revoke Screen Recording permission at any time in System Settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

For users in the European Economic Area: Pin Window: Rectangle Pro does not collect or process personal data. All app functionality operates locally on your device without any data transmission to external servers, fully complying with GDPR requirements.
