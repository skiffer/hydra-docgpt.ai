Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Captions AI Video

This Privacy Policy describes how Captions AI Video ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our iOS and macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Captions AI Video is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Data Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored only on your device:

  • Videos you import or record within the app
  • Speech transcriptions generated from your videos
  • Caption styles and customization preferences
  • Exported videos with captions
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Speech Recognition

Captions AI Video uses on-device speech recognition powered by the WhisperKit machine learning model. All transcription processing happens locally on your device. Your audio and video content is never sent to external servers for transcription.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Video or audio content
  • Transcription text or captions
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any form of behavioral data

1.5 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Captions AI Video Pro or make in-app purchases:

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID
  • Purchase receipts are validated locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework

2. Camera and Microphone Access

The App may request access to your device's camera and microphone for recording video with the built-in teleprompter feature. This access is used solely for recording within the app and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library to import videos for captioning and to save exported videos. We do not access, scan, or transmit any content from your photo library beyond what you explicitly select.

4. Third-Party Services

Captions AI Video does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain any ads.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your content is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption, passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
