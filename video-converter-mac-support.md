  <h2>Before contacting support</h2>
  <ol class="vc-steps">
    <li>Update Video Converter 1-Click from the Mac App Store.</li>
    <li>Check that your Mac has enough free storage for both the source and output files.</li>
    <li>Try a short file in MP4 format to determine whether the issue is specific to one source file or format.</li>
    <li>Choose an output folder where the app has permission to save files.</li>
  </ol>

  <h2>Common questions</h2>
  <h3>Why did conversion fail?</h3>
  <p>A damaged, incomplete or unusual source file may not decode correctly. Try playing the full source video first, then test another output format.</p>
  <h3>Why is the output larger than expected?</h3>
  <p>File size depends on duration, resolution, quality and the source. Select a lower resolution or quality preset when size matters.</p>
  <h3>Where are my videos uploaded?</h3>
  <p>They are not uploaded. Media processing happens locally on your Mac. See the <a href="/privacy-video-converter-1-click/">privacy policy</a>.</p>
  <h3>How do I get help with an App Store charge?</h3>
  <p>Apple processes App Store payments. Use Apple’s purchase history and support flow for billing or refund requests.</p>
