Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2024

Clean My Macbook

This Privacy Policy describes how Clean My Macbook ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

Clean My Macbook operates primarily on your local device. The following information is processed locally and never transmitted to our servers:

  • File system metadata (file names, sizes, dates) for cleanup analysis
  • System performance metrics (CPU, memory, disk usage)
  • Application data for the app management feature
  • Cache and temporary file locations

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal files or their contents
  • Browsing history
  • Passwords or credentials
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information from your device

1.3 Purchase Information

If you make a purchase through the App Store:

  • All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • We may receive anonymized transaction data from Apple for subscription management

2. How We Use Information

All data processing occurs locally on your Mac to:

  • Identify junk files, caches, and unnecessary data for cleanup
  • Display system health and performance metrics
  • Find duplicate files on your system
  • Analyze storage usage
  • Manage installed applications

3. Data Storage and Security

Clean My Macbook processes all data locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All operations are performed locally without cloud transmission
  • The app uses macOS sandboxing for enhanced security
  • We request only the minimum permissions necessary for functionality
  • Full Disk Access permission (if granted) is used solely for scanning and cleaning purposes

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution, in-app purchases, and subscription management
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing subscriptions and purchases

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All scanned data is displayed within the app
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data
  • Permissions: You can revoke Full Disk Access at any time in System Settings
  • Opt-out: You can use the app with limited functionality without granting Full Disk Access

6. Children's Privacy

Clean My Macbook is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@docgpt.ai
  • Website: https://docgpt.ai

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect personal information, these rights are automatically fulfilled.

10. International Users

Clean My Macbook processes all data locally on your device. No data is transferred internationally as we do not collect or transmit your data to any servers.

Summary: Clean My Macbook is designed with privacy in mind. All scanning and cleaning operations happen locally on your Mac. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.
