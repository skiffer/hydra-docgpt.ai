Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main difference between SlidesGo and Slides AI by DocGPT.AI?

SlidesGo is a template download service - you browse templates, download them, and manually customize. Slides AI by DocGPT.AI uses true AI to automatically generate complete presentations from your text or topic in seconds. It's the difference between automation and manual work.

Why is SlidesGo rated lower for value (3/5 vs 5/5)?

While SlidesGo is cheaper, users rate it poorly for value because: (1) manual template customization is time-consuming, (2) the pricing model is "not streamlined," and (3) download limits restrict heavy users. Slides AI by DocGPT.AI saves significant time with automation, justifying the higher price with 5/5 value rating.

Is SlidesGo cheaper?

Yes, SlidesGo costs only $23.99/year ($2/month), while our annual plan is $143.88/year. However, consider the time cost: if manually customizing templates takes 2+ hours per month, and your time is worth $60/hour, you're spending $120/month in time - making automation the better economic choice.

Which tool is better for frequent presentation creation?

Slides AI by DocGPT.AI is far better for frequent creation. We offer unlimited presentations with one-click generation, while SlidesGo limits you to 60 downloads/month and requires manual customization for each template. For agencies or regular users, automation wins.

Can I try before buying?

SlidesGo offers a free plan (5 downloads/month), while we don't currently offer a free trial. However, our Starter plan at $14.99/month (150 slides) is an affordable way to test true AI automation versus template downloads.

Is there a lifetime plan?

Yes! Slides AI by DocGPT.AI offers a lifetime plan for $249 (one-time payment) with unlimited presentations and all features forever. SlidesGo doesn't offer any lifetime option - only recurring subscriptions.

Which tool should I choose?

Choose Slides AI by DocGPT.AI if: You value your time, create presentations frequently, want full automation, need unlimited presentations, or prefer a lifetime payment option.

Choose SlidesGo if: You're on a tight budget, only need a few presentations per month, don't mind manual customization work, or want to try a free plan first.
