AI content vs human content: the real math

Research from Ahrefs found AI-generated content costs an average of $131 vs $611 for human-created content — about 4.7× cheaper. The smartest teams run a hybrid: AI drafts the bulk (titles, outlines, meta, first drafts) at a few dollars each, while humans edit and add expertise to the pieces that matter.

With GPT for Sheets you produce that bulk layer inside Google Sheets: drop a keyword in column A and generate titles, meta descriptions, outlines, and full drafts down the column with =GPT(), =CLAUDE(), and =SEO_BLOG() — programmatic and lean SEO at spreadsheet speed.

FAQ

How much cheaper is AI content than human writing?

Roughly 4.7× cheaper on average ($131 vs $611 per piece in one large study), though a publishing-grade workflow still budgets for a human editor on top of the raw generation cost.

Can I generate articles in Google Sheets?

Yes. With GPT for Sheets you write a formula like =SEO_BLOG("Best espresso machines 2026") or =GPT("Write a 1500-word article about " & A2) and drag it down to produce content for every row.

Is AI content good for SEO?

It works best as a hybrid: AI handles scale (drafts, meta, outlines), humans add accuracy, voice, and E-E-A-T. GPT for Sheets is built for the scale half — programmatic and lean SEO across rows.
