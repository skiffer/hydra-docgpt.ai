Privacy Policy

Save Story for Instagram - Media Downloader

Last Updated: November 14, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Save Story for Instagram. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension operates, what permissions we request, and how we handle your information.

Our Core Principle: We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data, browsing history, or downloaded content.

Extension Purpose

Save Story for Instagram has a single, clear purpose: to enable users to download publicly accessible photos, videos, stories, and reels from Instagram and Facebook with a single click.

The extension works by:

  • Injecting download buttons directly into Instagram and Facebook web pages
  • Extracting media URLs from publicly accessible web content when you click a download button
  • Initiating downloads to your local device using your browser's download functionality
  • All processing happens locally in your browser - no external servers involved

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be absolutely clear, our extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • ❌ Login credentials or authentication tokens
  • ❌ Browsing history or web activity
  • ❌ Downloaded media files or content
  • ❌ Instagram or Facebook account information
  • ❌ Location data or device identifiers
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Cookies or third-party tracking mechanisms

Local Data Storage

The extension stores minimal data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device and includes only:

Download Counter

A simple number tracking successful downloads (used to show rating prompts at specific milestones: 5, 25, and 45 downloads)

Rating Status

A boolean flag indicating whether you've rated the extension (prevents repeated rating prompts)

Important: This locally stored data is:

  • ✅ Stored only on your device
  • ✅ Never transmitted to any server
  • ✅ Not accessible to us or any third parties
  • ✅ Automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension
  • ✅ Can be cleared anytime through Chrome's extension settings

Permissions Explained

Our extension requests specific permissions to function. Here's exactly why each permission is needed:

1. Storage Permission

Why we need it: To save download counter and rating status locally on your device.

What we DON'T do: Access, read, or store any of your browsing data, cookies, or personal information.

2. Downloads Permission

Why we need it: To save media files (photos, videos, stories) to your downloads folder when you click the download button.

What we DON'T do: Access, view, modify, or delete your existing downloads.

3. Scripting Permission

Why we need it: To inject download buttons into Instagram and Facebook pages and handle download requests.

What we DON'T do: Modify page content other than adding download buttons, inject ads, or track your activity.

4. Tabs Permission

Why we need it: To open Instagram/Facebook in new tabs when you click "Open Desktop" or "Open Mobile" buttons.

What we DON'T do: Monitor your browsing, read tab contents, or track which websites you visit.

5. System Display Permission

Why we need it: To get your screen dimensions for properly sizing the mobile view window.

What we DON'T do: Capture screenshots, record screen activity, or access any visual content.

6. Declarative Net Request Permission

Why we need it: To modify User-Agent headers when opening mobile versions of Instagram/Facebook.

What we DON'T do: Block, redirect, or spy on your web requests.

7. Host Permission (all_urls)

Why we need it: To work on Instagram and Facebook domains (including various subdomains like i.instagram.com for API access).

What we DON'T do: Our code only executes on Instagram and Facebook - we don't interact with any other websites.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No external APIs or servers
  • No tracking pixels or beacons

The only external services accessed are:

  • Instagram API endpoints (i.instagram.com) - to retrieve publicly available media URLs
  • Facebook endpoints (facebook.com) - to retrieve publicly available media URLs
  • Chrome Web Store - when you click "Rate Now" in the rating prompt

These connections are made only when you explicitly click download buttons or rating links.

Data Security

While we don't collect any personal data, we implement security best practices:

  • All extension code is minified and optimized
  • Local storage uses Chrome's secure storage API
  • No external network requests except to Instagram/Facebook APIs for media URLs
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Open to security researchers for vulnerability reports

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal data whatsoever, our extension is inherently safe for all age groups. However, we recommend parental supervision for children's internet use in general.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over the extension:

View Permissions

Right-click extension icon → "Manage Extension" → "Permissions"

Clear Local Data

Chrome Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data → "Cookies and site data"

Disable Extension

Chrome Extensions → Toggle off "Save Story for Instagram"

Uninstall Extension

Chrome Extensions → Remove (all local data deleted automatically)
