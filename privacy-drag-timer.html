Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro

This Privacy Policy describes how Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our macOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

1.2 Data Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored only on your device:

  • Timer duration preferences
  • Pomodoro session settings (work duration, break duration)
  • Daily Pomodoro completion count
  • Theme preferences (light, dark, system)
  • Accent color settings
  • Menu bar icon style preferences
  • Click behavior settings
  • Full screen transparency settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing or search history
  • Any form of behavioral data
  • Timer history or patterns

2. How the App Works

Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro functions entirely offline and locally:

  • Drag-to-Set Timer: Timer durations are set locally through menu bar interaction
  • Pomodoro Timer: Focus and break sessions are tracked only on your device
  • Full Screen Mode: Timer display runs locally with no external connections
  • Settings: All preferences are stored in local app storage using macOS UserDefaults
  • Notifications: Timer completion notifications are handled by the local macOS notification service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected by Apple's built-in security measures:

  • All app data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • Data is protected by your device's encryption
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No internet connection is required to use the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses only the following Apple services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple StoreKit: For App Store review prompts (no data collected)
  • Apple UserNotifications: For timer completion alerts (with your permission)

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

5. Permissions

Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when timers complete (optional, can be disabled in System Settings)

You can manage this permission at any time in macOS System Settings > Notifications.

6. Children's Privacy

Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or external links beyond this privacy policy and support email.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems
  • All your preferences and data are stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • You can reset app settings within macOS by removing app preferences

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Since Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro does not collect any personal information, these rights are automatically fulfilled. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area: Drag Timer: Focus & Pomodoro does not collect or process personal data. All app functionality operates locally on your device without any data transmission to external servers, fully complying with GDPR requirements.
