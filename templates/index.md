Ready-made recipes for [GPT for Sheets](/gpt-for-sheets/): copy the formulas, drag them down your rows, done. Every template uses built-in AI functions — `=GPT()`, `=CLAUDE()`, `=GEMINI()`, `=PERPLEXITY()`, `=SERP()` — with no API keys required.

## E-commerce & content

- **[Bulk Product Descriptions](/templates/bulk-product-descriptions/)** — unique descriptions for 1,000 products with tone and keyword control.
- **[Bulk Translation](/templates/bulk-translation/)** — localize a catalog into 100+ languages with a two-formula matrix.
- **[Social Media Content Calendar](/templates/social-media-calendar/)** — a month of platform-specific posts from one `=GPT_TABLE()` call.

## SEO

- **[SEO Meta Titles & Descriptions](/templates/seo-meta-tags/)** — length-safe metas for hundreds of pages.
- **[Keyword Clustering & Intent](/templates/keyword-clustering/)** — turn a raw keyword export into a content plan.
- **[Programmatic SEO Pages](/templates/programmatic-seo-pages/)** — one row = one landing page; export to Webflow, WordPress and others.

## Sales & data

- **[Lead Enrichment](/templates/lead-enrichment/)** — company research, classification and emails; the spreadsheet-native Clay alternative.
- **[Data Cleaning](/templates/data-cleaning/)** — extract, normalize and classify messy imports without regex.

## How these work

1. [Install GPT for Sheets](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/gpt_for_sheets_docs_forms_slides/466607203252) from the Google Workspace Marketplace — the free tier is enough to try every recipe.
2. Open a template page, paste the formulas, adjust prompts to your data.
3. Generation runs at up to 10,000 results per hour; when done, use **Replace all GPT formulas with results**.
